Cool Cars Facts

By Chase McCracken

Cars have a cool history with lots of interesting facts.Karl Benz made the first gas-powered car in 1885, which was a three-wheeled vehicle.

Today, cars like the Bugatti Chiron Super Sport 300+ can go over 304 mph, making it the fastest production car.Some cars are also

incredibly luxurious, like the 1955 Mercedes-Benz 300 SLR, which sold for $143 million in 2022.There are also unique designs, like

the "American Dream" limo, which is over 100 feet long and even has a pool and helipad.


There are some surprising car facts too. Electric cars have been around since the 1800s, with many on the road by the early 1900s.

The Toyota Corolla is the most-produced car ever, with over 44 million sold since 1966. The smallest car, the Peel P50, was just 54 inches

long and 39 inches wide. Cars have also seen cool innovations like cruise control, first used in a 1958 Chrysler, and car radios, which

appeared in 1930 Cadillacs.

Car Stuff and Names

  • Most Expensive Car Ever Sold: 1955 Mercedes-Benz 300 SLR
